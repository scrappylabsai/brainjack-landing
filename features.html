Features

Your voice, typed everywhere

BrainJack turns your phone into a voice-powered AI keyboard for any computer. Not dictation — full content generation, keystroke injection, and multi-machine control from your pocket.

Join iOS Beta Install the Service
Core

Voice in, keystrokes out

Speak into your phone. Text appears on your computer — typed into whatever app has focus, as if someone were at the keyboard.

Real keystrokes

BrainJack types into the active window using native OS APIs — Windows SendInput, macOS CGEvents, Linux xdotool/ydotool. Not a paste. Real key-by-key input.

Clipboard mode

Don't have the service installed? The app works standalone — speak, AI generates, text copies to your phone's clipboard. Paste or share from there.

Private by default

On-device speech recognition. Your audio stays on your local network. No cloud relay, no third-party processing. Runs offline with a local ASR model.

Every OS

One service, one install command. Windows, macOS, Linux. Your phone connects over WiFi via WebSocket — fast, direct, no account needed.

AI-Powered

Agent mode — not dictation

Dictation apps type what you say. BrainJack understands what you mean and writes what you need.

Full content generation

"Write a professional email declining this meeting." BrainJack writes the whole email. Review it on your phone, edit if needed, send to your computer with one tap.

Knowledge base

Teach it about you — your writing style, your job, your context. RAG-powered personalization means better output the more you use it.

Conversational context

"I'm at the vet." ... "Write a letter confirming Buddy's rabies shot on March 15th." BrainJack carries context across prompts within a session.

Review before sending

Nothing reaches your computer until you approve it. See the output, edit it, then tap to inject. You stay in control.

"Having an AI that can generate a full email from a sentence, review it on my phone, then type it into Outlook on my work PC — that's the workflow nobody else offers."

Hardware

USB dongle — zero install on target

A small USB device that plugs into any computer and appears as a standard keyboard. No software, no drivers, no admin rights.

Works with anything

If it has a USB port, it works. BIOS screens, login prompts, legacy systems, air-gapped machines, hospital terminals, industrial equipment. The target sees a keyboard — nothing more.

Corporate AI bypass

Your company blocked ChatGPT and Copilot? The AI lives on your phone. The dongle is a standard USB keyboard to the work PC. No software to flag, no network traffic to detect.

How it works

Plug the dongle into the target machine. Open BrainJack on your phone. The phone connects to the dongle over Bluetooth. Speak, and the dongle types. The target machine never knows AI is involved.

Multi-System

One phone, every machine

Manage multiple computers from a single app. Switch between them instantly.

Device profiles

Each machine remembers its OS, macros, and auth token. Switch with one tap. BrainJack adapts keystrokes to the target platform automatically.

SSH terminal

Full terminal access from your phone. Run commands, manage servers, check logs. Landscape mode for real work.

Audio return

Your computer can speak back to your phone. Build alerts, deploy notifications, agent responses — anything that can HTTP POST can push audio to your pocket.

Command sheets

One-tap macro palettes for vim, tmux, emacs, VS Code, shell. Send complex key combos without remembering them. Color-coded and OS-aware.

Your Stack

Bring your own everything

BrainJack doesn't lock you into a cloud. Point it at your own infrastructure and keep full control.

Your ASR

Apple on-device, Whisper, Qwen — or any speech model on your own hardware. Your audio, your model.

Your LLM

Any OpenAI-compatible API. OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, vLLM, LM Studio. Or use our budget fallback.

Your network

Everything local. Phone to computer over WiFi. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking. HIPAA-friendly by design.
